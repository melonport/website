Melonport AG

Melonport AG was officially founded by Reto Trinkler and Mona El Isa in July 2016. It is a fully partner owned, private company domiciled in Zug, Switzerland. In February 2017, the company created a smart-contract which issued 599 400 MLN tokens and raising a total of CHF 2.5 million in under 10 minutes in exchange for the tokens. Melonport AG, the company, is solely responsible for the development of the Melon Protocol, the product. The company currently has 10 employees.

Melon is the first of its kind autonomous system. A system specific for the purposes of crypto token management. Both its frontend as well as its backend are hosted and executed on decentralised platforms. For the frontend it uses IPFS and for the backend it uses its own Domain chain connected to Polkadot. Using the newly develop WebAssembly standard it can be run as a zero config application from the convenience of your browser.

Governance

MLN token holders vote on a technical council. The technical council then votes on the next version of Melon. Melon has inbuilt incentives to ensure its ongoing development, makeing it free from centralised authoring.

Values, Mission, Vision

The Melon protocol is an open source project. We aim to build the most reliable asset management protocol using blockchain technology that is both inclusive and borderless. Users do not need to aquire permission for setting up funds on the Melon protocol. The Melon protocol is not owned by Melonport AG, it is intended as a public good, like the internet. As a result, Melonport AG does not take any fees and the protocol is free to use.
