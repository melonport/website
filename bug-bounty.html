MELONPORT AG BUG BOUNTY PROGRAM

DESCRIPTION

Melonport AG will launch a Melon fund on the Ethereum mainnet for a period of time which is expected to be approximately two weeks. The Melon fund smart contract deployed at address 0xMELONFUNDADDR, referred to as Bounty Melon Fund, will be funded with 500 Melon token by Melonport AG, and managed by the company.

BOUNTY CHALLENGE

In order to test the security of our smart contracts and thereby to detect possible bugs in its code, we invite and challenge our community to try and extract the 500 Melon token with technical means (hacking of software code) from the Bounty Melon Fund. In case all or a portion of the 500 Melon token could be extracted from the Bounty Melon Fund, the successful participant (“User”) in this bounty programme will be rewarded with the right to keep the 500 Melon token extracted from the Bounty Melon Fund (“Bounty Price”).

TERMS FOR BUG BOUNTY PROGRAM

Melonport AG is intended to be the only party that can send Melon tokens to the Bounty Melon Fund deployed for the purpose of this challenge. This Bounty Program does, however, not relate to User funds, tokens, virtual currencies or assets sent to the Melon fund by anyone other than Melonport AG for which Melonport AG takes no liability.

Melonport AG transfers all ownership rights to the Melon Token in the Bounty Melon Fund, i.e. the Bounty Price, to the User who successfully manages to extract Melon token through technical means (hacking of code) from the smart contract to an address under his control as a result of participating in this Bounty Program.

Melonport AG holds no liability towards any User funds, tokens, virtual currencies, assets or User time lost which results in a User loss with respect to this challenge. No tokens owned by any users are put intentionally at risk by Melonport AG.

In the event that User has critical information regarding the safety of the funds within the Melon smart contract which is different than extracting the funds from the contract (eg. locking the funds, destroying the funds, or other) - user should report the bug to team@melonport.com with the subject “CRITICAL BUG DISCOVERY”. In case the report is deemed valid by Melonport AG AND the vulnerability has not been executed, then Melonport AG may decide to reward the first person reporting this vulnerability with an amount up to 500 MLN, subject to passing Melonport’s KYC/AML policy for the proposed amount.

Melonport AG holds no reimbursements or liabilities whatsoever towards any funds, tokens, virtual currencies, assets or any other form of value which results in a loss in trying to achieve this bounty challenge.

The Bounty Melon Fund and this Bounty Program is temporary​ and can be shut down by Melonport AG at any time for any reason whatsoever and without prior warning. In this case, the Bounty Price will not be assigned to any User.

Only means of technical extraction using software directed at the Bounty Melon Fund are admitted as part of the Bounty Program. Any other means of extractions (i.e. any action which compromise the private key of Melonport AG used to hold and manage the shares of the Bounty Melon Fund, physical violence or other non-software related means) may lead to criminal prosecution.

Users are responsible for payment of all applicable taxes as per their country and jurisdictions. Individuals who are on US, Swiss, European (or other) sanctions lists or who are citizens or residents of sanctioned / embargoed countries (eg. North Korea, Iran, etc) may not participate.
